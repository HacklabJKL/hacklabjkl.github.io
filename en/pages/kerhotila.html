The lab

We host weekly club nights every Tuesday 18–21 and Saturday 14–17. The doors are open until the last key-carrying member leaves, which is often after midnight. Questions are best answered on our IRC-channel on Freenode, #hacklab.jkl or on our Telegram group.

Our lab space is located at Keskikatu 18 A, basement.

There are no parking slots on the yard but there's plenty of free parking in the neighbouring streets.

Entry via the yard, you'll see our sign and the doorbell.

If you have any trouble getting there, please call our lab phone: 041 807 9416.

We have plenty of tools and materials available for use.
